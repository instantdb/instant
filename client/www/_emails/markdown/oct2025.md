**Guest Authentication**

![](https://www.instantdb.com/img/emails/oct2025/s_6E082F007AE9AD0868C28574407BB3B75F0499E083CAED1B5165D941BDFD62C8_1762799995355_frame-1-bordered.png)

Instant now supports guest authentication! This allows your users to try your app before signing up and ensures they can keep all their data when they decide to create a full account with their email.

Use **`db.auth.signInAsGuest()`** to create a new guest user. This will create a new guest user with an id, but no email address.

When a guest user is ready to create a permanent account, you can use any of Instant's sign-in methods. The guest user will be automatically upgraded to a full user.

[Check out the docs](https://www.instantdb.com/docs/auth/guest-auth) to see a full example!

**Upgraded CLI**
