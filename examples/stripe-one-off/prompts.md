## 2. Mini Newsletter (Subscription)

A simple blog-style app where authors publish posts marked as free or premium. Anyone can read free posts. Subscribers ($5/month) unlock all premium content. Premium posts show a teaser paragraph with a paywall prompt for non-subscribers.

**Core screens:** Post feed (mixed free/premium), individual post view (with paywall for locked content), account/billing page.

## 3. Haiku Generator (Usage-based)

An AI-powered app that generates haikus from user-provided topics. Users purchase credit packs (10 credits for $2). Each generation costs 1 credit. The interface shows current balance, a topic input, and generated haiku output.

**Core screens:** Generator with credit balance display, purchase modal, history of generated haikus.
